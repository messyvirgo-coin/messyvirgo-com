The pattern from [March](/blog/2026/04/every-month-we-buy-more-of-the-same-token-you-hold/) continued in April: we pay real-world bills, and the result is that founders hold more **MESSY**, not less.

## What April Looked Like

Our April infrastructure costs were **1,300 USD** for AI agents, data, and core tooling.
Before any on-chain accounting:

- Michael had already paid **1,270 USD** on his personal card.
- Franco had paid **30 USD** on his card.

For the monthly bookkeeping:

- Franco injects **650 USDC** into the marketing wallet (half of the infra bill).
- From that wallet, Franco receives **1,000,000 MESSY (valued at 650 USD) and 30 USDC** to reflect his contribution.
- Michael receives **1,000,000 MESSY (valued at 650 USD) and 620 USDC**, reflecting that he had already fronted **1,270 USD** of the April infra bill on his personal card.

Vendors get paid in fiat, the DAO does not dump MESSY into external liquidity, and both co-founders end April with **more MESSY exposure** than they started with. Our costs remain a built-in DCA plan into the same token you hold.
