The pattern from [May](/blog/2026/06/every-month-we-buy-more-of-the-same-token-you-hold-may/) continued in June: we pay real-world bills, and the result is that founders hold more **MESSY**, not less.

## What June Looked Like

Our June infrastructure costs were **1,652 USD** for AI agents, data, and core tooling.
Before any on-chain accounting:

- Michael had already paid **1,370 USD** on his personal card.
- Franco had paid **282 USD** from his personal card/wallet.

For the monthly bookkeeping:

- Franco injects **544 USDC** into the marketing wallet (half of the infra bill minus 282 USD that he already paid from his personal card/wallet).
- From that wallet, Franco receives **1,400,000 MESSY (valued at 826 USD on Jul 4)** to reflect his contribution.
- Michael receives **1,400,000 MESSY (valued at 826 USD on Jul 4) and 544 USDC**, reflecting that he had already fronted **1,370 USD** of the June infra bill on his personal card.

Vendors get paid in fiat, the DAO does not dump MESSY into external liquidity, and both co-founders end June with **more MESSY exposure** than they started with. Our costs remain a built-in DCA plan into the same token you hold.
