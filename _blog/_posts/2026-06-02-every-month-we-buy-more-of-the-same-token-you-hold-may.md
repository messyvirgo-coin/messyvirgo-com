The pattern from [April](/blog/2026/05/every-month-we-buy-more-of-the-same-token-you-hold-april/) continued in May: we pay real-world bills, and the result is that founders hold more **MESSY**, not less.

## What May Looked Like

Our May infrastructure costs were **1,100 USD** for AI agents, data, and core tooling.
Before any on-chain accounting:

- Michael had already paid **1,071 USD** on his personal card.
- Franco had paid **30 USD** on his card.

For the monthly bookkeeping:

- Franco injects **520 USDC** into the marketing wallet (half of the infra bill minus 30 USD that he already paid with his card).
- From that wallet, Franco receives **828,000 MESSY (valued at 550 USD on Jun 1 and Jun 2)** to reflect his contribution.
- Michael receives **858,000 MESSY (valued at 550 USD on Jun 2) and 521 USDC**, reflecting that he had already fronted **1,071 USD** of the May infra bill on his personal card.

Vendors get paid in fiat, the DAO does not dump MESSY into external liquidity, and both co-founders end May with **more MESSY exposure** than they started with. Our costs remain a built-in DCA plan into the same token you hold.
