## How to Earn Raffle Tickets

### DEX Buy Route

To earn raffle tickets, you must buy $MESSY on a **Decentralized Exchange (DEX) on Base**. Only DEX buys count. Centralized exchange (CEX) buys, OTC transactions, and wallet transfers do **not** generate tickets.

**Minimum per transaction:**

Each individual DEX buy transaction must be worth at least **$100 USD** in $MESSY to generate tickets. Buys of $100 or less generate zero tickets.

**Ticket formula (per transaction):**

For each qualifying DEX buy transaction:

- If `buy_usd ≤ 100` → **0 tickets**
- If `buy_usd > 100` → `tickets = buy_usd − 100`

**Examples:**

- Buy $350 → 250 tickets
- Buy $120 → 20 tickets
- Buy $90 → 0 tickets

**Unlimited qualified buys per wallet:**

A single wallet can make unlimited qualifying DEX buys above $100 during the campaign. Each qualifying transaction generates its own ticket bucket. There is no cap on the number of qualifying buys a wallet can make.

**Net new MESSY:**

We look at all your on-chain DEX buy transactions for $MESSY on Base during May 1–31, 2026. If you sell $MESSY during that period, the tickets for the "sold" portion may be reduced accordingly. Only net new $MESSY that remains in your wallet generates full tickets.

## Eligibility Requirements

To remain eligible for the raffle, a wallet must satisfy **both** conditions below at the June 15, 2026 snapshot.

### 80% Hold Requirement

Each wallet's **peak MESSY balance** during May 1–31, 2026 is recorded.

At the June 15, 2026 snapshot, that wallet must still hold at least **80% of its peak MESSY balance**.

**Example:** If your highest balance in May was 10,000 MESSY, you must hold at least 8,000 MESSY at the June 15 snapshot.

If the balance falls below 80% of that peak, the wallet is **disqualified** from the raffle.

### Net Buyer Requirement

A wallet must be a **net buyer** of $MESSY during the campaign period (May 1–31).

- Your total DEX buys of $MESSY in May must be **greater than or equal to** your total $MESSY sells and transfers out during the same period.
- If you sell or transfer out more $MESSY than you buy → you are **not eligible**, regardless of tickets earned.
