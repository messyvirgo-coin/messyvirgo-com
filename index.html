Long-Term Vision

We're not building just another AI bot. We're training an on‑chain fund agent that can stand alongside human managers and be measured in real time.

Messy's long‑term vision is an AI that can scan the noise, surface real opportunities, and execute disciplined on‑chain strategies within parameters the community can see and shape.

Today that looks like rails: data, tools, and test funds. Over time, the goal is a live engine where performance, risk, and decision‑making are visible on‑chain, with $MESSY as the asset that connects users to the agent's trajectory and the ecosystem around her.

Who our funds are for

Our current funds on Guru are small, human‑managed test portfolios designed for people who:

  • • Want to allocate a slice of their crypto portfolio into higher‑growth assets,
  • • But don't want to chase every new narrative, token, and rotation themselves.

Instead of managing dozens of individual positions and transactions, you hold a single fund token that represents a share of the underlying basket. When the fund adjusts its positions, your exposure updates automatically without you having to trade every leg.

These funds are experimental and small by design. They help us test ideas in real markets and generate data for Messy's future strategies, rather than being the final product.

A 4.95% tax on $MESSY trades funds development, operations, and community incentives. This is a token‑level mechanism on $MESSY trades and does not apply to the assets inside our funds.
