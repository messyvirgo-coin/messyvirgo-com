Creator Wallet Security & Transparency

The original creator allocation (431.85M $MESSY) has been transferred from a single externally owned address into six Safe smart wallets. This was necessary because MESSY's 1.25% reflection mechanic does not work with standard third-party locking services, which would either fail or mishandle the reflection tax on transfers.

These six Safe wallets collectively hold all team and treasury allocations -- there are no undisclosed team wallets. Every transfer from these wallets is taxed at 1.25% and movements are reported in monthly "State of the Treasury" updates so anyone can verify activity on-chain. What is Messy Virgo Coin (MESSY)?

Messy Virgo Coin (ticker: MESSY, commonly written as $MESSY) is an AI-focused crypto project on Base. The roadmap progresses from culture and narrative (Phase 1) to an agentic due diligence engine (Phase 2), then to transparent, constraint-based portfolio execution (Phase 3), and finally to community-led governance (Phase 4).

What are the tokenomics fees?

MESSY uses a 4.95% transaction tax designed to fund holders and development:

  • 1.25% reflections: distributed to holders automatically.
  • 3.70% treasury: funds AI development, marketing, and strategic burns/buybacks.

How do reflections work?

Reflections are an automatic holder reward mechanism: a portion of each trade (1.25%) is shared across holders. In practice, balances can increase over time simply by holding (subject to on-chain mechanics and trading activity).

How is the treasury managed (and why Safe wallets)?

The creator allocation has been transferred into six Safe smart wallets on Base. This approach is used because the reflection mechanic can be incompatible with traditional third-party lockers. Treasury and team movements are intended to be transparent and verifiable on-chain, with recurring disclosures (‚ÄúState of the Treasury‚Äù) and detailed schedules in the litepaper.

All transfers from these Safe wallets are still subject to the same 1.25% reflection tax.
