FAQ

What is Messy Virgo Coin (MESSY)?

Messy Virgo Coin (ticker: MESSY, commonly written as $MESSY) is an AI-focused crypto project on Base. The roadmap progresses from culture and narrative (Phase 1) to an agentic due diligence engine (Phase 2), then to transparent, constraint-based portfolio execution (Phase 3), and finally to community-led governance (Phase 4).

What is the official contract address?

The Base contract is 0x09f87f948c88848363b124c9099cbb58e4cc7cb6. Always verify on BaseScan before swapping.

What are the tokenomics fees?

MESSY uses a 4.95% transaction tax designed to fund holders and development:

  • 1.25% reflections: distributed to holders automatically.
  • 3.70% treasury: funds AI development, marketing, and strategic burns/buybacks.

How do reflections work?

Reflections are an automatic holder reward mechanism: a portion of each trade (1.25%) is shared across holders. In practice, balances can increase over time simply by holding (subject to on-chain mechanics and trading activity).

How is the treasury managed (and why Safe wallets)?

The creator allocation has been transferred into six Safe smart wallets on Base. This approach is used because the reflection mechanic can be incompatible with traditional third-party lockers. Treasury and team movements are intended to be transparent and verifiable on-chain, with recurring disclosures (“State of the Treasury”) and detailed schedules in the litepaper.

All transfers from these Safe wallets are still subject to the same 1.25% reflection tax.

What are $MESSYBASED and $MESSYFUND?

The ecosystem includes two complementary funds:

  • $MESSYBASED (Base): a fast-moving innovation lab to test strategies in a low-cost environment.
  • $MESSYFUND (Ethereum): an Ethereum-based fund whose value is linked to a portfolio of assets; it is the target vehicle for Phase 3 portfolio execution.

What’s the roadmap in plain English?

  • Phase 1 (LIVE): culture engine and identity.
  • Phase 2 (Q1 2026): agentic token due diligence engine (signal detection + structured analysis).
  • Phase 3 (Q3 2026+): autonomous trader for portfolio management under transparent constraints.
  • Phase 4 (2027): DAO transition—governance and treasury control shift toward $MESSY holders.
