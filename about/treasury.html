Treasury, Safes & Vesting

The creator-side $MESSY allocation is documented through six Safe smart wallets and related BaseScan transactions on Base. This page brings together wallet addresses, allocation amounts, vesting rules, unlock schedules, and monthly treasury movements so holders can verify custody and activity in one place.

How to read this page

The Safe wallets show where the tokens are held. The allocation table explains what each wallet is for. The vesting schedule explains when tokens may unlock. The monthly treasury updates show actual movements after setup. Together, these give holders one place to verify both planned allocation and real on-chain activity.

Governance and execution are separate but connected: token holders steer major decisions, the Swiss association provides the real-world execution layer, the Safe wallets hold the tokens on-chain, and vesting controls when allocation buckets may move.

Governance decides

Token-holder governance is the intended path for major budgets, partnerships, protocol changes, and long-term treasury direction.

Association executes

The Swiss association gives the project a legal execution layer for services, contracts, stewardship, and approved operational decisions.

Safes hold

The creator-side allocation buckets under ongoing custody are held in six Safe smart wallets on Base for public, verifiable custody.

Vesting controls timing

Each allocation bucket follows a published unlock schedule. Transfers from the Safe wallets remain subject to the same 1.25% reflection tax.

Safe Wallet Custody

The original creator-side allocation was split from a single externally owned address into public Safe wallets and related transfer transactions on Base. These Safes collectively hold the published team, contributor, liquidity, community, and treasury allocation buckets under ongoing custody; completed direct transfers, such as upfront tranches, are linked separately where applicable.

We use Safe smart wallets instead of a standard third-party locker because $MESSY includes a 1.25% reflection mechanic. Traditional lockers can be incompatible with automatically changing token balances or can mishandle reflection-taxed transfers. Safe custody keeps the allocation buckets public and verifiable while preserving the token's normal transfer rules.

All transfers from these Safes remain subject to the same 1.25% reflection tax applied to regular transfers. Movements are documented below in monthly State of the Treasury updates.
