Week of December 22–28, 2025

Marketing

  • Clarified public positioning of the Agentic Due Diligence Engine, emphasizing an agent-native, lens-based intelligence layer rather than a traditional "AI trading dashboard," and prepared a full website content update to align messaging with the upcoming internal milestone, Phase 2 (Minimum Viable Intelligence), and the planned launch of the Macro Economic Lens.

  • Enforced quality standards in Telegram by disqualifying approximately 20 accounts abusing raid and reward mechanics, reinforcing fair participation and long-term community integrity.

Business & Operations

  • Initiated a third-party smart contract audit and team verification process with an external security provider to strengthen security assurances, transparency, and long-term credibility.
  • Completed an external security review with GoPlus Labs confirming the Base contract is renounced, with limited exclusions and no remaining warnings, and requested removal of the legacy warning from CoinGecko.
  • Monitored post-LP routing and execution behavior across liquidity venues to ensure stable, predictable trade quality and reliable routing for users.

Product Development

  • Implemented incremental improvements to the Technical Analysis Lens, including broader chain coverage, improved primary pool selection, and more robust handling of multiple data providers.
  • Validated the full Technical Analysis pipeline (Input → Data → Info → Insight → Output) to improve reproducibility, auditability, and graceful degradation under partial data failure.
  • Advanced the Macro Economic Lens by developing a regime-scoring framework, iterating on a comprehensive macro report, and mapping macro regimes into practical allocation bands for crypto portfolios.
