Creator Wallet Security & Transparency

The original creator allocation (431.85M $MESSY) has been transferred from a single externally owned address into six Safe smart wallets. This was necessary because MESSY's 1.25% reflection mechanic does not work with standard third-party locking services, which would either fail or mishandle the reflection tax on transfers.

These six Safe wallets collectively hold all team and treasury allocations -- there are no undisclosed team wallets. Every transfer from these wallets is taxed at 1.25% and movements are reported in monthly "State of the Treasury" updates so anyone can verify activity on-chain. Full vesting schedules, CTO allocations, and all planned unlock dates are in the Litepaper.
